Environmentally Conscious Company, Samsung C&T

Why this is important

Samsung C&T who has led the minimization of environmental impact and maintenance of ecosystem, puts the role of company for future generation first.

How to manage this

Samsung C&T is actively involved in the worldwide reponse to climate change as a pressing global concern, implementing carbon management on a global scale and reducing an energy use through the construction of environmentally-friendly buildings.

Our Activities

The leading company in global carbon management

Samsung C&T has been included in “Hall of Fame – Gold Club” for 2 consecutive years since 2017 Carbon Disclosure Project (CDP) assessment, for its response to climate change and performance of disclosing carbon information. Samsung C&T has been selected as the best company in carbon management for 6 consecutive years since 2012; and will continue to make an effort for GHG reduction as a leading player in global carbon management.

Energy conservation through environmentally-friedly buildings

Samsung C&T E&C Group has been pursuing the environmentally-friendly building certifications based on the competencies in energy efficiency technology. For Samsung Electronic DS Headquarters Office Project, Samsung C&T obtained the LEED* Certificate with Silver rating and also for IFC2 Tower and Rasuna Tower Projects in Indonesia, it obtained the Gold plus rating and Gold rating, respectively. Besides, for Godeok city-operated apartment project in Korea, it secured the best rating of G-SEED* Certificate. Now Samsung C&T has acquired ten domestic and international environmentally friendly certifications, transforming itself into a leading environmentally-friendly corporation.

* LEED(Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) is a globally-recognized third-part certification program developed by the US Green Building Council (USGBC). The program examines 72 items and assigns three different ratings, platinum, gold and silver.

* G-SEED(Green Standard for Energy and Environmental Design) is a green building certification system used in Korea. The system assigns ratings in 7 specialized areas including water cycle management, materials and resources.

Safety, Our First Priority

Why this is important

Samsung C&T put the safety as the priority value that the enterprise must protect.

How to manage this

Samsung C&T operates the safety training facilities for employees customers according to the management policy that puts the safety in priority. In addition, Samsung C&T strengthens the accident prevention checking system that reflects the characteristics of each business groups such as construction equipment, attractions etc..

Our Activities

Raising the safety awareness of employees and customers through experience-oriented training

The E&C Group of Samsung C&T is operating “Safety Academy” for its employees and partners. By providing training programs in 4 major experience centers (site/fire/emergency/earthquake), the company takes the initiative in enhancing safety awareness and accident prevention.

The Resort Group operates 'Lenny's Safety Center' which is the theme-based experience program for daily safety, emergency responses, disasters, etc. This center is composed of earthquake experience, simulation game, emergency rescue, and escape from fire where every customer who visit Everland can learn how to respond various situations.

Prevention of critical disasters by enhancing the safety of site equipment

Given that the risk of safety accidents remains high at construction sites, it is crucial to prevent major accidents by responding to near misses in a timely manner. In particular, the E&C Group learned that a series of near misses involving equipment has a great impact on the construction projects by causing major accidents, delays in construction, and increases the safety management level by checking the response system, drawing the improvement tasks, and enhancing the monitoring and information sharing.

Establishing the integrated attraction monitoring system

The Resort Group has created the world's first comprehensive monitoring and accident prevention system to ensure attraction safety. The system monitors the operations of rides, manages trends, sets alarms, and performs troubleshooting to prevent malfunction and identify the causes of problems with the control system once they occur; thus minimizing the attraction's loss of operation time.

Basic compliance

Why this is important

Corruption is a serious problem that can have a wide-range impact on the entire society and there is growing social demands for integrity and transparency in corporate management.

How to manage this

Samsung C&T is improving its compliance system by establishing compliance standards and taking proactive actions based on regular inspection and customized education.

Our Activities

Raising the level of compliance risk management

Samsung C&T has been making efforts to proactively prevent noncompliance by establishing an integrated system to classify compliance risks, and raising the level of corporate compliance management. Samsung C&T classified compliance risks into 11 categories including fair trade, information protection and subcontracts that are used selectively by the 4 groups according to their business characteristics

Stronger compliance at overseas operations

  • Samsung C&T T&I Group has carried out compliance inspection on its overseas operations every year and operated the system to cope with potential risks proactively.

    The Fashion Group established a new compliance system in Shanghai Corproation and set compliance guidelines that incorporated major Chinese laws.

Customized compliance education for employees

  • Samsung C&T Fashion Group offered education on dealership laws for sales personnel and education on subcontracts, anti-corruption and technology for employees working in the fields of purchase and production. Education on topics directly related to specific jobs is provided to small groups of employees, and case studies

    including discussions have been introduced as a part of the education for a greater effect. Samsung C&T Resort Group offered education on major controversies surrounding copyrights and trademarks to new emplpoyees and personnel in charge of compliance.

Sense of Community for Mutual Growth

Why this is important

With the new management paradigm of co-prosperity and cooperation, Samsung C&T is making efforts for mutual growth with its suppliers.

How to manage this

Samsung C&T remains committed to promoting a culture of fair and transparent transaction and to helping suppliers grow, by implementing a variety of programs for shared growth.

Our Activities

A system for fair trade and shared growth

Samsung C&T is taking steps to prevent unfair trading behaviors that may occur in the process of negotiating and executing contracts. Samsung C&T E&C Group discloses the results of evaluation in real time when selecting contractors, to ensure transparency of evaluation. Also, it set up and operates dispute-resolution procedures to resolve conflicts that may arise in subcontracting transactions.

In addition, Samsung C&T is implementing a variety of shared growth programs to provide suppliers with financial, technical, and educational support so that they become more competitive, and operating Partner’s Day as a win-win place to share the excellent performance and contribute to the creation of common benefits.

Spreading the fair trade and culture of shared growth

Samsung C&T continuously improves its systems and processes to enhance the fairness of the contracting process.

Samsung C&T E&C Group is making efforts to settle the fair trading culture by disclosing the supplier registration process and supplier selection and management standards through Partners Portal, a supplier registration and management system.

Samsung C&T Fashion Group is working on expanding -- to secondary suppliers -- the shared growth programs that previously focused on primary suppliers. In 2016, Samsung C&T E&C Group signed agreements for fair trade and shared growth with 48 primary suppliers and 67 secondary suppliers to create a fair, transparent transaction environment for all suppliers and spread a culture of shared growth across the broader fashion industry.

New Opportunities for All

Why this is important

Samsung C&T does not perceive the rapidly changing external environment as a crisis but an opportunity and is focusing on various business opportunities.

How to manage this

In the response of climate change trend, Samsung C&T is looking for ties between climate change and new potential business opportunites by taking advantage of its E&C and T&I Groups. In addition, the Fashion and Resort Groupa are linking evolving IT trends, growth of the service industry, and changing customer demands brought about by the 4th industrial revolution to the development of new products.

Our Activities

Turning the crisis of climate change into business opportunity

Samsung C&T E&C Group won a contract to build the world's largest Tees Biomass CHP(Combined Heat & Power) Plant in the UK which gave it the opportunity to seure the technology in renewable energy project. Based on the experience in this project, Samsung C&T E&C Group plans to participate in the growing biomass power plant projects continuously.

Samsung C&T T&I Group signed a long-term PKS* supply contract with Biomass Power Plant in Japan, based on its know-how and network built through the operation of the palm tree farm and palm oil trade in Indonesia. This contract marks the first step into Japan's biomass makret and a meaningful achievement since it can lead to more supply contracts with other biomass power plant across Japan in the future.

*PKS(Palm Kernel Shell) : refers to the shell fractions left after the nut has been removed. It is used as fuel instead of fossil fuels at biomass power plants.

Reinforced animal and plant contents

The Everland of Samsung C&T Resort Group offers contents using animals and plants to create values of healing, communication and experience to customers at the same time. During winter season, the park opens Snow Safari, which shows how animals survive in winter, and the Butterfly Park, which features storytelling wherein the ecology and characteristics of butterflies are explained. In spring, the Panda World displays flower panda in connection with flowers, and Lost Valley opens a flower exhibition hall to delight the visitor's sense. In addition, Music Garden, which is designed to allow visitors to experience the healing effects, serve as a natural learning place.

Development of new products to meet the 4th Industrial Revolution

The Fashion Group has rolled out innovative products including 'Sol Bag' , which uses sunlight to charge mobile phones, and 'Welt' that uses IoT technology to allow the wearer to collect data on his or her daily routine and personal activities since the release of 'The Humanfit', a wearable platform brand that combines IT technology and fashion.

Sharing Beautiful Values

Why this is important

Samsung C&T pursues the environment that the enterprise and community grow together by practicing the genuine and authentic sharing.

How to manage this

Samsung C&T realizes the value of sharing through the integrated social constribution activities of 4 business groups and the volunteer works that each business group is doing with communities.

Our Activities

Building the integrated social contribution system

Samsung C&T established the integrated social contribution system that all business groups such as engineering & construction, trading & investment, fashion and resort participate together and is performing the social contribution activity focusing on 3 areas : future generation (individual), community (community) and environment (earth).

The enterprise pursuing the coexistence with communities

  • Samsung C&T E&C Group has been involved in improving the living environment for local communities both at home and abroad. In 2017, Village No. 2 was built in Chargaon, India, followed by Village No. 3 in Nga My and Ban Dat, Vietnam. On the domestic front, Samsung C&T employees repaired and improved 15 houses of low-income families in Gangdong-myeon, Gangneung-si, Gangwon-do, significantly improving the quality of life of the local residents.

  • The Trading & Investment Group launched Safe Communities and Safe House as a first step in serving the local community. The T&I Group carried out the urban beautification project dubbed "Transformation of Container Boxes" in order to improve the environment of high-crime areas in the local community and make the community safe in Songpa-gu, Seoul. Safe House is a program that provides actually needed support to improve the residential environment for senior citizens living alone.

  • The Fashion Group of Samsung C&T has run “Heart for Art” programs since 2013, to support art education for visually-impaired children as well as children and adolescents from low-income/multicultural families. The “Heart for Art” develops curriculum that combines fashion and fine art. It guides students to learn fashion with five senses and to feel it in their daily lives. The programs also help participants boost their self-confidence and get to know more about beauty.

  • "Hope Keepers", a volunteer group of the Resort & Park Services Group, takes advantage of ifeguards who work at the water park Caribbean Bay to provide underwater rehabilitation therapy for disabled children in the local community. Employees and disabled children are paired to perform safe rehabilitation therapies that fits the needs of individual children, such as swimming and underwater exercise.
