Basic Principles

Samsung C&T aims to make an organizational culture where all employees and executives share our corporate values and practice integrity.
Therefore, we enact Samsung C&T Code of Ethics based on Samsung Management Philosophy and Samsung Spirit as a code of conduct and standards for value judgement that all employees shall follow and uphold.

Pursuit of Co-prosperity (WIN-WIN)

We seek co-prosperity where employees, clients, partners, shareholders, communities, country, and humanity can prosper together.

Fair Competition (FAIR)

We comply with laws, regulations and ethical standards of the countries and communities where we operate, and pursue free and fair competition based on our competence.

Humanity, Ethics, Manners, and Etiquette (BASIC)

We create our future on the global stage, following basic principles and keeping humanity, ethics, manners, and etiquette.

Self-Judgement and Voluntary Practicing

  • We make our own judgement about our behaviors based on the following standards, and practice Code of Ethics voluntarily in order to save the honor of ourselves and the Company.
    • A) Is a behavior against the law?
    • B) Is a behavior in compliance with Samsung Code of Ethics?
    • C) Is a behavior against conscience?
    • D) Does a behavior benefit or harm any person unfairly?
  • We point out each other’s wrongful behaviors that go against the Code of Ethics and correct ourselves. When an employee or an executive cannot make a judgement on a behavior on his/her own, he/she should report the behavior to his/her seniors, Ethics Committee, HR, Auditing Team, or Legal Team.

Ethical Management

Samsung C&T respects customers’ opinions and protects their assets and honor by upholding quality-oriented management. We also compete freely and fairly, complying with the laws, regulations and business ethics of all domestic/overseas sites in which we operate.

Ethical Business Operation
  • We do not exploit loopholes in the laws and/or regulations or conduct unfair business activities that go against business ethics.
  • We take competition as an opportunity for mutual growth, and respect lawful rights of our competitors.
  • We pursue fair competition, and do not take advantage of our competitors’ weak points unfairly or conduct any activity that stifles free competition.
Customer Satisfaction
  • We provide best products and services to meet the demands and expectations of our customers, and respond quickly and attentively to customers’ rightful claims.
  • Quality is our prime value that cannot be compromised.
  • We put quality and public safety as our top priorities in all of our judgement and actions.
  • We strive to secure the upmost competitiveness in the best and fastest manner and at the lowest cost.
  • We protect assets, intellectual properties, and trade secrets of our customers as carefully as our own.
Fair Trade
  • We provide all qualified businesses with a fair chance to bid to become our partner.
  • We select partner firms in a transparent and fair manner based on the quality, price, and reliability of the goods and services they provide.

    - We set clear evaluation criteria for partner selection and secure fairness by strictly applying the criteria.

  • We make fair trades with our partners on an equal footing, and discuss conditions and procedures with them in advance.
  • We put the Company’s honor and interest first in doing business with our partners, and do not receive any money, gifts or benefits in exchange for favors.
  • We do not abuse the Company’s power and dominant position to take any unfair advantage.
  • We support our partners in various ways, and do not shift our burden onto them.
  • We explain the purpose and spirit of Samsung Code of Ethics to our partners, and make a joint effort to form clean and transparent trade practices and ensure a fair trade.

Basic Ethics of Employees

Employees of Samsung C&T keep basics and principles and have humanity, ethics, manners, and etiquette in our everyday life to safeguard our self-esteem and honor. We pursue mutual growth of individual employees and the Company by initiating positive changes.

Basic Ethics
  • We have pride and self-esteem as representatives of Samsung C&T and keep the honor with appropriate clothing, polite behavior, and graceful language.
  • We perform our duties fairly and keep the promises that we make. We do not lie, make excuses, drag others down, or commit any unethical acts.
Completion of duties
  • We share the goals and values of the Company, and perform our duties faithfully following the management policies of the Company.
  • We do our best for every assigned task and perform our duties fairly. We comply with the law and the Company’s rules and policies.
  • We understand our assigned duties and roles clearly. We act based on our own judgement within our discretion and take responsibility for our actions.
  • We respect creative ideas and suggestions from our colleagues and other departments, and exchange opinions with senior and junior co-workers openly and freely.
Transparent and Fair Performance of Duties
  • We perform all our duties fairly, faithfully, and with integrity, and always strive to foster a sound and clean organizational culture.
  • We protect physical/intellectual properties and trade secrets of the Company, and never use the Company’s assets for personal interest.
  • We do not commit any misconduct such as embezzlement, breach of trust, and bribery. When there is a conflict between personal and corporate interests while engaged in the Company’s business, we put the Company’s interest first.
  • We do not engage in unethical acts that can be socially criticized both at work and in our private life.
  • We do not manipulate or exaggerate facts for personal gains or interest of the team, and do not hide important information or keep it private.
Mutual Respect between Employees and Executives
  • We keep business manners with our co-workers and refrain from slander and malicious behaviors against other employees or executives.
  • We do not offer any presents to senior employees, executives or to other departments.
  • We do not make any financial transaction with other employees or executives.
  • We do not form factions or discriminate against other employees or exeutives based on educational background, sex, religion, kinship, or regional ties.
  • We do not make unfair/unreasonable requests to other employees or executives.
Prevention of Sexual Harassment in the Workplace
  • We strictly refrain from any act that can be perceived as sexual harassment, understanding that sexual harassment in the workplace undermines motivation and productivity of the victim and ultimately harms the Company by damaging the Company’s image or leading to lawsuits.
Management of Information and Security
  • We do not disclose classified information such as trade secrets and information on new businesses without prior approval or authorization.
  • We assign a manager for important documents so that the information is closely managed and not leaked to outsiders.
  • We do not use illegal software downloaded from the internet or other channels.
Management of Safety and Risk Prevention
  • All employees have a responsibility to promote safety of the Company.
  • We strive to prevent safety accidents. We report safety-related risks and take relevant measures immediately upon finding them.

    - We take every possible measure to address emergency situation such as disaster.

  • We comply with the laws, regulations and standards on safety, and strictly observe safety rules in every circumstance.

Responsibility to Employees and executives

Samsung C&T respects dignity and value of individual employees and executives, and endeavors to improve emotional/physical quality of life of employees and executives.

Human Dignity
  • Samsung C&T treats individual employees and executives with respect and dignity.
  • Samsung C&T is committed to ensuring that all employees and executives take pride in their work based on a sense of ownership.
  • Samsung C&T establishes a system where employees and executives can perform their duties in a rightful manner, and provides necessary education and support.
Nurturing and Protecting Talents
  • Samsung C&T provides necessary support to help employees and executives faithfully perform their duties with strong sense of ethics, and nurtures and protects creative talents by providing opportunities for self-improvement and growth.
  • Senior workers nurture their junior workers as proactive and competent talents, and provide necessary advice and guidance considering junior workers’ aptitude and interests.
Fair Evaluation
  • Evaluation and treatment of employees and executives shall be fair and impartial regardless of one’s gender, educational background, religion, regional ties, disability, and marital status.
  • Fair evaluation and compensation shall be made based on the achievement, capability, efforts, and contribution of each employees and executives.
  • We keep basics and standards, and recognize the value of honest mistakes, and promote rewards than punishments.

Responsibility to our country and Society

Samsung C&T protects shareholders’ profit and contributes to bringing prosperity to people’s lives and driving social development by growing into a sound company through reasonable business activities.

Corporate Responsibility and Rights
  • We respect cultures and traditions, abide by laws and regulations, and contribute to community development in every place which we operate.
  • We diligently fulfill our duties to the nation and community and pay our fair share of taxes.
  • We reject any corrupt activities and irregularities that hinder sound business operation.
Respect for Shareholders
  • We provide stable long-term profit to our shareholders through reasonable investment and sound management.
  • We put efforts to reduce cost and improve productivity to generate stable long-term profit.
  • We respect shareholders’ rights to information, rightful demand, suggestion, and official decision, and secure transparent management to establish mutual trust with shareholders, and record and manage our financial status based on Generally Accepted Accounting Principle (GAAP).
Contribution to Social Development
  • We create new values for our nation and society through relentless innovation and R&D.
  • We provide everyone with equal opportunity for employment regardless of educational background, gender, religion, and regional ties.
  • We believe that we have Corporate Social Responsibility to our society and actively engage in voluntary work, disaster relief, and social enlightenment.
  • We humbly accept/embrace rightful demands brought forward by every rank of society and community and put efforts to resolve the issues.
Environmental Protection
  • We make our utmost effort to prevent pollution and improve environmental conditions for the conservation and protection of the clean environment, and comply with the laws and regulations on environmental protection.
  • We do our best to prevent unnecessary exploitation of natural resources.
  • We do not perform business activities that hurt the environment, and do our best to prevent pollution and contamination.

Compliance with Code of Ethics

  • We establish Guidelines for Ethical Practice regarding bribery and other misconducts so that our employees and executives can strictly comply with the Code of Ethics.
  • We reward employees and executives who comply with the code of ethics and contribute to the Company’s ethical management, and penalize those who violate the code of ethics.
  • Employees and executives shall consult HR executives or Ethics Committee when questions arise regarding the interpretation of or compliance with the ethics code/practice guidelines.
  • When there is no relevant provision on the Code of Ethics or on the Guidelines for Ethical Practice, or when there is a room for dispute in interpreting them regarding the Company’s management, decisions and interpretation of the Ethics Committee shall be followed.
  • The Code of Ethics is the foundation for the ethical management of the Company and has precedence over other corporate rules.
